What are Hackos?
Hackos are hackerrank currency you can earn and spend on the site

How do I get Hackos?
Everyone starts of with 100 Hackos, and gets hackos for solving challenges, and maintaining a login streak. For challenges the hacko points earned are based on the level of the challenge.

You also get hackos for maintaing a login streak, where a streak of 1 day = 1 hackos, 2 days = 2 hackos, and so on with an upper limit of 10.

Where can I use Hackos?
You can currently use Hackos to buy test cases, with each on at 5 points for seeing tricky corner cases.

Is that all?
